What is an omelet?
-- An omelet is a dish made with egg(s).
What are the ingredients for an omelet?
-- Eggs
-- Butter
-- Pan
-- Fire for cooking
How to make an omelet?
-- Put a pan on fire or stove. 
-- Put butter in the pan and wait for it to heat up.
-- Put eggs into a bowl or cup and stir it untill the egg white and yolk are blended together.
-- Put the blended egg in the pan and bake it untill golden color.
